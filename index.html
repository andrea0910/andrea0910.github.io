About

I didn't plan my way to product. I followed what interested me until the path made sense.

Finance gave me a way of thinking in incentives. CS showed me that vision without implementation goes nowhere. Teaching ethics made clear the hardest calls are rarely the technical ones.

My CS and MS&E degrees were two different ways of asking the same question: how do you make good decisions with incomplete information? I grew up in Paraguay, studied in the U.S., worked in Korea and Miami. Every new context required me to build understanding from scratch. No playbook, just observation and iteration.
