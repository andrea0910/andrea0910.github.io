Remote
US Strategy & Operations Intern
Bucketplace (Ohouse)

Ran user interviews for a Korean home decor platform entering the US market. The need is the same everywhere: people want a home that feels right. What changes is how they search, what they trust, what signals quality, and what reads as foreign. The job wasn't to translate the product. It was to understand why the decisions look different.

May – Aug 2024
Miami, FL
Investment Department Intern
Santander Private Banking International

Built a neutral interest rate model using historical rate data, inflation indicators, and productivity trends. It gave the portfolio team a basis for forming their own view on upcoming Fed adjustments, independent of market consensus. Most of the work was in the translation: turning a regression into something a portfolio manager could actually use in a client meeting.

Jun – Aug 2023
Seoul, South Korea
Strategic Investment Operations Intern
Hanwha Life

Mapped 20+ GenAI products across Asia and North America for Hanwha Life's investment committee. Personalized ESG investing was already standard in the US. Korean clients were only beginning to explore it. The variable that mattered wasn't which product was better. It was where each market was in its adoption curve, and whether the fit was actually there yet.

Jun – Aug 2025
Stanford, CA
Course Assistant — The Ethical Analyst

Course assistant for Stanford's ethics seminar. Most people just haven't named them yet. Teaching made me faster at naming them earlier.
